About Us

Top Choice For Children

Kito Model School was founded in 2015 by Mrs Kambine Rosette Ankunda and Madam Kateita Joseline, sisters inspired to offer affordable yet quality education to young ones in Bweyogerere division, Kampala. We offer both Nursery and Primary education with a broad, balanced curriculum and strong co-curricular programs.
